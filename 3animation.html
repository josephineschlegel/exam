OnBoarding Document

For this assignment, we took on team roles that we had previously learnt about in the Basix UX theme, along with creating a team canvas and mapping out ideas. Our goal for our game was to create an educational, but user friendly, welcoming game for students thinking of moving to Copenhagen. We had an illustrator character explain the different steps one must complete after moving, as we all agreed that it wasn’t clear for us personally once we had moved. The team dynamic was much better in this group than any before, as we had all experienced what not to do at this point.

Onboarding

For our Onboarding game, we were vastly stressed for time. Unfortunately, all my other team members fell ill during this week, therefore we didn’t have much meeting around what we could change or how to do it. As we mostly communicated through text, I learnt the importance of team communication and meeting in person, as we largely misunderstood each other at times. My job for this was copywrite all of the text and find the relevant questions and information, along with documentation and presenting the game during the exhibition. The game shows some issues that we didn’t have time to resolve, for example the fact that once you click on an item to showcase the information, you must click on the item again to go to the next page and get rid of the speech bubble, it would’ve been a better user experience if they had the option to click anywhere. Also, the design of the quiz at the end could’ve been stylized and designed better. We also received feedback from users that the text was too long and there was too much information.
